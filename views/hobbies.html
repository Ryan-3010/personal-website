My Hobbies

Entreprenuership

I am drawn to entrepreneurship because it sits at the intersection of creativity, problem-solving, and impact. I enjoy identifying inefficiencies, taking calculated risks, and building solutions from the ground up—much like structuring an investment or designing a system that scales. Entrepreneurship allows me to combine my interests in finance and technology while constantly learning, adapting, and taking ownership of outcomes.

Backcountry Skiing

Backcountry skiing appeals to me because it demands preparation, discipline, and respect for risk. Every decision— from route selection to snowpack assessment—has real consequences, forcing a balance between ambition and caution. Being deep in the mountains sharpens my judgment, reinforces humility, and rewards thoughtful decision-making, which mirrors how I approach challenges in both academics and leadership.

Golf

Golf interests me for its blend of strategy, patience, and mental resilience. Success isn’t about perfection on every shot, but about managing mistakes, adjusting to conditions, and staying focused over long periods of time. The sport has taught me the value of consistency, self-awareness, and long-term thinking—skills that translate directly to business, finance, and leadership.
