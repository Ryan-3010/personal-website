Ryan Falender

Third-year Dean’s Leadership Fellow and Honors student at the University of Colorado Boulder’s Leeds School of Business, majoring in Finance with a minor in Computer Science.

Ryan is deeply curious about financial markets, risk management, and strategic investment decision-making, while also exploring how machine learning and coding can solve complex problems. He currently serves as Vice Chairman of the Finance Board in CU Student Government, overseeing a ~$33 million budget impacting over 30,000 students.

Outside the classroom, Ryan is a volunteer head coach, practice player for the CU Women’s Basketball team, and an avid mountaineer and skier pursuing all of Colorado’s 14,000-foot peaks.
