Recent Projects

During Startup Summer, I helped build BuildJoy, a startup designed to make LEGO building more accessible and creative by transforming a single image into a step-by-step LEGO instruction booklet, with the option to purchase the exact pieces needed to complete the build. What started as an early concept evolved through rapid prototyping, customer validation, and iteration into a clear, scalable product at the intersection of creativity and technology. I was closely involved in refining the value proposition, shaping the business model, and developing a concise, compelling pitch that communicated both the technical innovation and market opportunity. Presenting BuildJoy to a panel of judges sharpened my ability to articulate complex ideas under pressure, and finishing third overall in the competition validated both the originality of the concept and the strength of our execution.

During the Google Impact Hackathon, I collaborated on an energy consulting project centered on improving residential energy efficiency in Colorado. Our team developed a concept integrated into the MyColorado app that would help homeowners better understand and optimize their home energy usage through accessible data and targeted recommendations. The experience was fast-paced and high-energy, requiring rapid ideation, technical feasibility checks, and impact-focused thinking. It showed me how technology, policy, and user-centered design can come together to create scalable solutions for real-world problems.

I am currently working on an ongoing golf simulator consulting project with the University of Colorado, where I’ve been deeply involved in research, planning, and stakeholder coordination. I’ve met with school directors, vendors, and advisors to evaluate feasibility, costs, and long-term value, while preparing analyses to support data-driven decisions. The project has required strong organization and communication as I balance multiple perspectives and constraints. Through these meetings and preparations, I’ve gained hands-on experience navigating ambiguity and aligning diverse stakeholders around a shared objective.
